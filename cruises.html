FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

The sunset dinner cruise (6:00 PM) is most popular as you get to watch the stunning Goa sunset from the river. The season runs from October to May, though cruises operate year-round.
The afternoon party boat (3 PM) is family-friendly. The night party boat (8 PM) is adults only (18+). Please mention if you're bringing children when booking so we can recommend the best option.
No swimming ability is required. Life jackets are provided to all passengers. Non-swimmers can still participate in snorkeling with our guided support.
Outside food is generally not allowed as meals are included in the package. For private yacht charters, we can accommodate special dietary requests in advance through our chef.
Free cancellation up to 48 hours before departure for a full refund. Cancellations within 48 hours will receive 50% refund. No refund for no-shows. In case of bad weather, we reschedule at no charge.
